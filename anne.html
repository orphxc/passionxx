Anne with an E

Creator: Moira Walley-Beckett

"Anne with an E" is a Canadian series based on the beloved novel *Anne of Green Gables*. It follows the imaginative and fiery Anne Shirley as she navigates her new life at Green Gables, challenging societal norms and finding her place in a world that often misunderstands her.

"It's not what the world holds for you, it’s what you bring to it."

Originally Aired: 2017–2019

About the Creator

Moira Walley-Beckett is an Emmy Award-winning writer and producer, known for her work on *Breaking Bad* and *Flesh and Bone*. With *Anne with an E*, she brought a fresh, empowering take to a literary classic.
