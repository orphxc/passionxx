Hanya Yanagihara

"A Little Life follows four friends, Willem, Jude, JB, and Malcolm, through their lives in New York City as they navigate relationships, career challenges, and the lasting trauma that each of them carries. About the Author

Hanya Yanagihara (born 1974) is an American novelist and editor. She is best known for her novel *A Little Life* (2015), which was shortlisted for the Booker Prize and won the Kirkus Prize for Fiction. Yanagihara is also the editor-in-chief of *T Magazine* and has been recognized for her writing and exploration of trauma, survival, and human endurance in her work.
