About the Author

Stephen Chbosky (born January 25, 1970) is an American film director, screenwriter, and author. He is best known for writing the bestselling coming-of-age novel The Perks of Being a Wallflower (1999) as well as for writing and directing the 2012 film adaptation of the book.
