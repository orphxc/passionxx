Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Developer: Moonton

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a 5v5 multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Moonton. The game features team-based battles, strategy, and a large variety of heroes to master. It has become a cornerstone of the mobile esports scene, with international tournaments and millions of players worldwide.
