Homeschool (Thai Film)

Director: Somchai Prasert

"Homeschool" is a poignant Thai drama that explores the life of a young Thai student who faces the challenge of being homeschooled after experiencing personal trauma. With strong cultural ties, this story delves into the importance of family, personal growth, and education in the face of adversity. A powerful narrative on the strength of resilience and the Thai educational system.

"True education goes beyond the classroom. It’s about nurturing the heart."

Released: 2023

About the Director

Somchai Prasert is a highly respected Thai director and screenwriter, known for his ability to blend emotional depth with social commentary. His films often explore familial relationships, personal identity, and the complexities of life in modern Thailand. "Homeschool" is a testament to his deep understanding of Thai culture and his keen ability to tell emotionally compelling stories.
