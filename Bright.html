About the Author

Jennifer Niven's first young adult novel, All the Bright Places, was released in 2015. The narrative follows two teenagers, Violet and Finch, who are struggling with mental health issues.

It won a 2015 Goodreads choice award for Best Young Adult Fiction and was longlisted for the 2015 Guardian Children's Fiction Prize.

It has been adapted into a film by Netflix and stars Elle Fanning, Justice Smith, Keegan-Michael Key, Alexandra Shipp, and Luke Wilson.
