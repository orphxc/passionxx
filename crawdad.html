About the Author

Delia Owens (born 1949) is an American author, zoologist, and wildlife conservationist. She is best known for her debut novel *Where the Crawdads Sing*, which became a bestseller and received critical acclaim for its poignant storytelling, rich description of nature, and exploration of loneliness and resilience. Before becoming a writer, Owens spent many years in Africa studying wildlife.
