Monster (2023)

Director: Hirokazu Kore-eda

"Monster" (怪物) is a 2023 Japanese drama film that intricately explores the perspectives of a mother, a teacher, and a child. Through masterful storytelling and emotional depth, the film unveils a powerful message about misunderstanding, truth, and empathy.

About the Director

Hirokazu Kore-eda is an acclaimed Japanese director known for his deeply humanistic films. With works like "Shoplifters" and "Nobody Knows," he continues to move audiences around the world through delicate storytelling and emotional honesty.
