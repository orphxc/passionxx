About the Author

Holly Jackson (born 1992) is a British author of mystery novels. She is best known for her A Good Girl's Guide to Murder series.

Holly Jackson grew up in Buckinghamshire, near Oxford in England, and wrote her first novel when she was 15 years old.

It has been adapted into a film by Netflix and stars Elle Fanning, Justice Smith, Keegan-Michael Key, Alexandra Shipp, and Luke Wilson.
